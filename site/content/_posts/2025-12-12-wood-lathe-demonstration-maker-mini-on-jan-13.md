MAKE Roanoke is thrilled to host another FREE Maker Mini, this time a demonstration on the Wood Lathe! This class will be on Tuesday, January 13 from 6-8 PM.

Join us to learn about basic operations on safely using a wood lathe including components, chucks, cutting tools, and changing the gears and speed of the lathe. Watch a demonstration as we turn a few pieces on our lathe!

This class is suitable for beginners, and is open to those age 16 or older. Minors must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian. This event is open to the public, you do not need to be a MAKE Roanoke Member to attend. 

Space is limited, so attendees MUST register on the [Meetup Event! ](https://www.meetup.com/make-roanoke/events/312410646/?eventOrigin=group_events_list)
