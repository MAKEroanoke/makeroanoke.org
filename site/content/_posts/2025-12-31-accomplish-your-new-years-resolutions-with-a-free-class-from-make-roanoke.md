Sign up for a membership at MAKE Roanoke in January and get a free class* in our New Year's Resolutions promotion! 

If your resolutions include things like learning new hobbies, making new creative friends, expressing your creativity more, learning how to make or repair something, or even volunteering at a local non-profit, MAKE Roanoke has your back! 

Come tap into your creative self at our community makerspace, where we have the tools to make just about anything! We have a well equipped wood shop, metal shop, fiber arts studio, 3d printers, electronics station, and a laser cutter is coming soon! 

Our community is a vibrant, collaborative group of creative people of all crafts, and are very welcoming to new makers of all skill levels. MAKE Roanoke is entirely run by volunteers, so there are ample volunteering opportunities from helping improve our facility to organizing events and more!

\*Terms: 

* For new MAKE Roanoke members only
* Value is up to $50, and can be used as a discount on a more expensive class
* Void if Membership is cancelled before redemption
* Class Coupon Expires 12/31/2026
* Limit: One redemption per member
* Gifted Memberships are not eligible
