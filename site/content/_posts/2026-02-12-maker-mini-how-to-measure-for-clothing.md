Join us at MAKE Roanoke in this FREE Maker Mini about how to measure your body for clothing! 

We will cover how to properly measure your body and then how to utilize those measurements when shopping online or constructing your own clothes. 

Every attendant will have the option to have their body measured as part of the class!
