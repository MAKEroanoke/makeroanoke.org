MAKE Roanoke is hosting a fundraiser at Taziki's Ivy Market on Sunday, February 15 from 4-9 PM!

Dine in at the Taziki's Ivy Market location and mention MAKE Roanoke, and a portion of your purchase will go directly to benefit our non-profit makerspace. You can also support us by ordering online at [Tazikis.com](http://tazikis.com/) or through the Taziki’s app. During checkout, simply select the **"Spirit Night"** pop-up to add it to your cart and ensure your order benefits our fundraiser night.

MAKE Roanoke has made over $700 thanks to restaurant charity fundraisers like this one. We appreciate your support!!
