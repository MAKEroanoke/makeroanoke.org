Join us at MAKE Roanoke for another FREE Maker Mini!

This lecture will focus on how wood naturally moves and how to account for this movement when designing wooden projects. We'll focus on material choice, glueing methods, and different ways of attaching tabletops.

This Maker Mini is recommended for those age 12 and up, and it is suitable for beginner to intermediate woodworkers.
