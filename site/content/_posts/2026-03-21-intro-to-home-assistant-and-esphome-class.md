MAKE Roanoke is hosting our first class on Home Automation using Home Assistant!

In this class you'll learn the basics of the Home Assistant smart home platform:

* What it is
* What it's used for
* Basic Configurations

Additionally, students will get hands-on experience creating a custom smart home device with ESPHome by making a Home Assistant compatible temperature and humidity sensor.

Students are required to bring a laptop with the Google Chrome Browser installed, and a USB-A port. You canÂ [download Google Chrome here.](https://www.google.com/chrome/)

This class is open to the public, you do NOT need to be a MAKE Roanoke member to attend. It is suitable for beginners, and is recommended for ages 16 and up.

The cost to attend is $45, and the cost for MAKE Roanoke members is $35. MAKE Roanoke members can also bring one guest at the discounted member rate!

Seats are limited, so please [RSVP on the Meetup Event](https://www.meetup.com/make-roanoke/events/313432537/)
